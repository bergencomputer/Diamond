About DHD Hardscapes

DHD Hardscapes is a locally owned and operated hardscape contractor based in Cave Creek, Arizona. Licensed, bonded, and insured for both residential and commercial work, we pride ourselves on building outdoor environments that stand the test of time while elevating curb appeal and livability.

From the first consultation to the final sweep‑down, we blend craftsmanship with transparency—meaning you’ll always know what we’re doing, why we’re doing it, and how much it costs.
